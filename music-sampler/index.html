Relient k has been my favorite band since the sixth grade. My friend, Tyler, gave me the CD of their album "Mmhmm." "Mmhmm" is one of their most popular albums. Their most famous song, "Be My Escape," comes from that album. I've really enjoyed growing up with this band and watching how they've evolved over time. I'm excited for what the future holds for them!
